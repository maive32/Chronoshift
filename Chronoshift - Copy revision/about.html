About

Time safari, Inc provides an extraordinary hunting service to anyone interested. The special part is the company allows hunters to travel back in time to hunt creatures that have died long ago. We can travel to location or time.

Techonolgy

The time machine relates to Einstein’s theory general theory of relativity. We can’t share all our secrets but the time machine’s technology is state of the art technology that can’t be found any where else.

History

We started 55 years ago by a passionate hunter and businessman named Henry Rand. He wasn’t satisfied with many years of hunting in Africa, he wanted to hunt the most dangerous game of all time, the Tyrannosaurus Rex. He worked with a scientist to make the time machine a reality. 30 years later, we’re still the only company with access this technology.
