Woolly mammoth

Description: The Woolly mammoth is an extinct species of elephant, alive during the Pleistocene period and becoming extinct in the Holocene epoch. The Woolly mammoth was the last line of mammoth species, being a diverge from the steppe mammoth. It is a closet relative to the Asian elephant. The Woolly mammoth are roughly the size of modern African elephants, males reaching 8.9 ft-11.2 ft while females reached 8.5-9.5 ft tall.
