Rules

Time travelling is risky business, not only to the hunters but to everyone else. Rules have been in place place to protect everyone, a hunter’s failure to follow them could result in death to the party and might change the future. Time Safari must preserve the present by preserving the past. Time Safari inc, invites customers to read the rules before booking a hunt.

1. Follow the guides' instructions

Listen to the guides’ instuctions, they know what they are doing. When an instructor will tell a hunter when to shoot, when to not shoot, and etc. Following these instructors are essential to avoiding danger and preserving the future. The punishment for disobeying the guides is an additional fee of 10,000 dollars plus possible government intervention.

2. Stay on the path

No hunter should step off the path, any action can result in a change in the timeline so be careful. This action can be done by mistake. If someone is in danger, they can easily walk off the path so it’s important to avoid that no matter the situation.
